Validation of information accuracy

Metamask enables transparency and immutability that, in turn,
allows financial institutions to validate the trustworthiness of data present in the DLT platform.
The decentralized KYC process acts as a streamlined way for gaining secure and swift access to up-to-date user data.
This lowers the labor-intensive efforts that an institution puts behind gathering information.
